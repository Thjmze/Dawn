She also identified Raher Solnari as the paranoid creator of the Khoda and the island's various contingencies. Following this meeting, the party used a fire sphere to teleport to a humid underground laboratory filled with ciphered primordial texts and a humanoid creature in stasis that bore a striking resemblance to Errata. Beezel used the stone tablet on the stasis specimen, causing it to turn into ice and reveal clear coordinates on their map for the next contingency. Before departing the lab, the party salvaged several spell scrolls, including a buffed version of Negative Image, while Star remained uneasy about the laboratory's apparent connection to Errata's origins.
