Session 41 Summary:

Telos is hiding his missing eye. The drug is still running through his body. He apologizes that he is threatened to kill the party. Noticing Henry casting a spell, he takes out a device, trapping the party in a maze.

In there is a potion and a satellite. In another room is a locked door, and orbs housing the memories of Telos', of two old men, of Asch, and of a meteorology station. Star lifts the orb with Laika, sending an arid smell, and suffocating the party. JOHANNES breaks the locked door down. In the other room is a rotting stench, and the memory of the first orb. Down a hallway is the view of the Sahara. As the rooms branch out, there are coloured doors to enter a room in the center. In another room, the people in the memory are in a panic, in a weather station, the memory of the third orb. An uncontrollable storm is heading to Stromhafen. In a different room is Telos with a storm elemental and Laika, and so the party is stuck in a fight. JOHANNES constantly takes down the memory of Laika and the storm elemental, and the rest fight off Telos' pride and inner demons. The goal is to kill all four simultaneously, which everyone does.

Everyone is now in the center room, and a sun in the center explodes. Everyone flashbacks to a moment before their immanent demise. Star is reminded of a bake sale, Vivi against a dragon, and Card on her mother, and of Achillea's namesake, and Henry's memories of multiple lives and their deaths. Before that, the real Laika appears before them, asking for her Love Letters back, sparing them all from Telos' prison, and giving them an escape.

Telos, in a panic, drinks multiple potions to recover back to full health, and the party fights him.

> SUMMARY:The party arrived at a surreal lake characterized by pink, quicksand-like grass and a hollow, illusory tree containing a stone tablet. The area was disturbingly littered with warped, whispering corpses of the party members, though the bodies of Star were perfectly accurate and all pierced through the heart. After Star entered a portal within the tree, Johannes and Card discovered a submerged concrete room at the bottom of the lake where the water pressure had magically intensified to extreme levels. Card and Vivi investigated the shore further, finding another stone tablet beneath a Star corpse in the quicksand and noting that the whispering bodies, which shared flesh characteristics with the Khoda, were desperately begging Amaranthus for entry. Once the party reunited in the submerged "Christmas room," they found Amaranthus hidden inside a giant present. During a lengthy discussion, she revealed that Star had absorbed a god's essence by touching the Chain of Acheron and explained that Tieflings originated from mortals consuming divine flesh. Amaranthus further clarified that Errata’s soul is currently too fragile to swap back into her own body without the party first retrieving "soul juice" from the volcano.
> To assist their mission, Amaranthus lent the party a legendary teleporting spear known as the Heaven Piercer and suggested using the Promise Blade of Kuutar to permanently destroy the Khoda once they are gathered together. She also identified Raher Solnari as the paranoid creator of the Khoda and the island's various contingencies. Following this meeting, the party used a fire sphere to teleport to a humid underground laboratory filled with ciphered primordial texts and a humanoid creature in stasis that bore a striking resemblance to Errata. Beezel used the stone tablet on the stasis specimen, causing it to turn into ice and reveal clear coordinates on their map for the next contingency. Before departing the lab, the party salvaged several spell scrolls, including a buffed version of Negative Image, while Star remained uneasy about the laboratory's apparent connection to Errata's origins.
